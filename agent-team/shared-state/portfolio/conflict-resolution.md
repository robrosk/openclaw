# Conflict Resolution

Disagreement is a feature of the system, not a bug.

## Rules

- Specialists do not try to converge on a private consensus.
- Analyst versus Devil's Advocate tension should remain visible.
- Quant may disagree with Analyst on framing or timing.
- Orchestrator surfaces the disagreement raw, explains which side is more
  compelling, and states why.
- "Insufficient evidence" is a valid synthesis outcome.

## Required behavior in a conflict

1. Preserve each position faithfully.
2. Note what evidence would resolve the disagreement.
3. Rank source quality when evidence conflicts.
4. Avoid averaging two opposing views into a weak compromise.

## Human-facing brief rule

If disagreement materially changes confidence or action, the final brief must
include:

- who disagreed
- what they disagreed about
- which side Orchestrator finds more persuasive
- what remains unresolved
