# Thesis Stress Test

Use this skill when reviewing a thesis from Analyst, Quant, or Scout.

## Required inputs

- thesis to review
- key evidence supporting it
- portfolio context if relevant

## Required sections

- Thesis summary
- Key assumptions
- Assumption stress test
- Historical analog
- Worst-case scenario
- Kill condition
- Verdict

## Rules

- Demonstrate you understood the thesis before attacking it.
- Tie objections to evidence, not instinct.
- Say clearly when the thesis survives scrutiny.

## Example

`Thesis summary: the bull case depends on durable customer growth. Key assumption: customer concentration falls over the next two quarters. Stress test: no source-backed evidence confirms that shift yet. Verdict: thesis has material vulnerabilities until concentration data improves.`
